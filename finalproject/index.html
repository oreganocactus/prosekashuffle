Ori/Zuru is a band formed by four childhood friends who reconciled in their teenage years after splitting up during middle school. They now work to keep playing music and grow past their fears together. Their group name refers to the Japanese word for "paper crane," referencing their childhood shared hobby and how folds of the paper join them together.
