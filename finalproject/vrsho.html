VR SHOWDOWN is a virtual performance group which makes use of virtual reality spaces and avatars to construct elaborate mysteries for their fans to solve. Each member plays a virtual "persona" during these performances, taking on over-the-top personalities to create a chaotic but memorable show. As the group grows closer, the members slowly escape their warped views on emotions and relationships to find their own happiness. The group name references their virtual reality performing space and the dramatic aspects of their shows.
