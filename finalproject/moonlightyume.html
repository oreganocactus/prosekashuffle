Moonlight Yume is an idol group made up of two sisters who are trying to find their identities as individuals, a lost child pageant star seeking out her emotions, and a rookie trying to find her place on-stage. The four of them seek to redefine what it means to be idols in their own way, unbound by expectations. Their group name translates to "Moonlight Dream," referring to the dream they seek off the beaten path.
