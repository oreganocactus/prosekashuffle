Fire Up!! Tori!!! is a stage performance troupe formed by Ootori Emu, whose family owns the amusement park they perform at. They work to bring joy and happiness to amusement park goers, forming closer bonds and learning to move past their insecurities as they move from coworkers to friends. Their group name refers to rising again from the ashes of a fire, similar to a phoenix. "Tori" is in reference to the Japanese word for bird/phoenix, symbolizing the rebirth of their desired selves while they perform.
